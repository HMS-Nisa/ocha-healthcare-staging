Heart bypass surgery, also known as coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG), is a major procedure used to treat coronary artery disease. For many international patients, Malaysia has become a top destination due to its world-class medical facilities.

### Why Choose Malaysia?
Malaysia's healthcare system is renowned for its high standards. Hospitals like **Sunway Medical Centre** and **IJN** are equipped with the latest technology.

* **Cost Savings:** Up to 70% cheaper than Singapore.
* **No Waiting Lists:** Immediate scheduling.
* **Language:** Doctors speak fluent English.
